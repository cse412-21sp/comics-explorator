How Trends in Comics Around the World Reveal Our Society

By Rodion Dovhan, Johnny He, Yinzhou Wang

Comic books are still very popular around the world, but in the recent decades there has been a shift as to what the readers like more

Firstly, let’s take a look at the global sales

Global comics market

We can see that the digital comics are much more popular in Japan than in the US. In fact, Japan’s digital comics revenues are bigger than the revenue from print and digital in the US combined

Why such a big difference you ask?

Asian countries have larger digital comics platforms because their societies are more technological

Also, Manga - a unique Japanese comics style that is very popular, not only in Japan, but around the world

Popularity Trends in the US

Manga has been overtaking American Comics even in the US

The sheer difference in the magnitude of numbers with which Manga is outselling Comics speaks volumes

..so why do people seem like Manga more now?

To answer that we should first look at the gender rations in Comics and Manga

As we see Manga has a noticeably bigger ratio of females to males. That is because Manga targets larger demographics. While American Comics mostly targets males, Manga also targets females with Josei and Shojo genres. Besides that Manga doesn’t only include stories about superheroes, but also sports, detectives, fantasy, comedy, etc.

In other words, Manga is much more diverse and appealing to a broader demographics
